Creditor
--------
1. ownership key (string)
2. # of months (int)
3. monthly payment (# btc, list-int)
4. creditor public address (string)
5. buyer name/id (string/int)
6. day of month (list-string)

Buyer
-----
1. previous transaction hash (string)
2. transaction index (int)
3. private key (string)
